ABOUT ME
Cyclist Safety App
After being given the challenge to develop an app utilizing the Data.Nashville.gov database I, along with two team members, designed an app dedicated to the safety needs of local cyclists.
The Cyclist Safety App is an app that provides peace of mind for cyclists by gathering all their safety resources and providing in one application.
PROBLEM STATEMENT
Provide Nashville’s cyclists with an easily accessible resource that helps ensures their own safety along with that of fellow riders.
TARGET MARKET
We started our research by defining a target user. After analyzing the database, we saw a lot of potential in designing for a local Nashville cyclist.
INTERVIEW INSIGHTS
To gain insight, we interviewed two Nashville locals who biked on a daily basis.
"My bike is the way I familarize myself with the city. I never look up places to go. I just go on bike rides to discover what is around me."
- Ronnie
"A major concern I have is that my friends and family often times worry about my safety while I'm cycling."
- Dejon
• Cyclists had many digital resources readily available. We found that we really don't need to design another app that helps locate new hotspots or maps a path for cyclists. They are completely satisfied with the resources they have.
• They expressed frustration with poor infrastructure on Nashville’s side roads. Users wanted an easy way to report roadside hazards.
• There seemed to be a lack in urgent safety resources. With safety being the key topic, cyclists requested customizable app for when they are in times of need.
SOLUTION
CYCLIST SAFETY APP
The Cyclist Safety app is designed to streamline the otherwise cumbersome process of finding safety information in times of need.
MEDICAL RECORDS
Users are able to easily track their medical records. This information also may be accessed on the lock screen in case of emergency.
URGENT CONTACTS
Local emergency numbers are provided with the option to add other emergency contacts. Users are also able to add urgent group messages and easily alert all their loved ones of their situation.
REPORT HAZARDS
To report road hazards, the hubNashville contact information is provided with the option to send the current location of the hazard.
NEARBY CLINICS
For non-emergency injuries, data.Nashville.gov provides nearby clinc locations and information.
