Vibe
This was my midway capstone project for the Front-End Design and Development Bootcamp. In this project I applied both my user experience design and front-end development skills. Vibe is an application which simplifies the process of creating a collaborative playlist.
PROBLEM STATEMENT
Develop an efficient way to create collaborative playlists at social gatherings
COMPETITIVE ANAYLSIS
Existing Collaborative Playlist Applications Either...
Require Spotify Premium
Spotify Premium is the main music source where users are able to create and listen to their custom playlists at any point in time. All outside source collaborative playlist applications require a Spotify Premium account.
Have a Complex Flow
Existing methods of creating collaborative playists requires all users to share the same music source. They also require a complex process, such as text mesage or emailing the playlist from user to user.

After surveying 25 people, I was able to analyze user's music source choices. My results included a wide variety of music sources, verifying that the chances of a group of people all sharing the same music source is not probable. The majority of the users said listed unpaid music sources. Lastly, despite the popular require of Spotify Premium, only 3/25 people used Spotify Premium.
